Investigation

Sherlock has an easy to use investigation feature , where you can add all your investigation be it from your bookmarked logs , filter logs or even from your result generated from find query .Further you can format the entries according to your choice and even print the investigation in any format.

Timeline

With our timeline feature you can place your investigation notes in a chronological manner and it will display in a timeline format for your quick glance enabling fast investigation .

Printing

With our printing feature you can copy all your notes , bookmarks , investigation at a one glance either to your clipboard or can be printed to any format you want. We provide you the facility to highlight the desired information in investigation by quick formatting and even you can attach links or images to the undergoing investigation.

Advanced Filtering

Our intuitive filtering interface allows you to quickly sort through logs for specific investigations. It offers powerful options like similarity, equality, and regex. You can also group multiple columns for advanced filtering.

Find

Feature, you can perform accurate searches in your log files using both simple keywords and regular expressions (regex). This powerful combination allows you to pinpoint specific events, errors, or patterns efficiently.

Bookmark

You can simplify log analysis and investigation by marking and save crucial reference points within log files. With a simple click, you can create virtual flags that act as signposts for important events, errors, or data entries. These bookmarks enable quick navigation to specific sections of interest, eliminating the need for manual scrolling or time-consuming searches through extensive logs providing seamless collaboration among team members, as bookmarks can be easily shared, aiding in faster troubleshooting and more efficient decision-making.

Advanced Parser

Sherlock has an automated log detection and parsing feature. Additionally, it provides a robust parser setup interface to create rules for parsing business-specific logs. The parser can handle both single-line and complex multiline logs.

Log View Customization

You can even customize which column you want to hide or to be pinned in while scrolling through large log files , it provides you the flexibility to rearrange columns for a better view.

Customizeable Theme

Easily toggle between Dark and Light theme for smoother view.
